Slide 5: Climate Risk Hotspots
Slide number: 5
Content:
  Risk drivers:
    - Rising temperatures shifting suitable altitude (~300 m per °C)
    - Rainfall variability and drought stress
    - Pest/disease pressure (coffee leaf rust, berry borer)
  Hotspots:
    -
      Region: Central America (1,000–1,600 m)
      Risks: Rust resurgence, drought; suitability loss by 2050
    -
      Region: Ethiopia highlands
      Risks: Suitability migration upslope; biodiversity trade-offs
    -
      Region: Brazil (Minas Gerais)
      Risks: Heat waves, irregular rainfall; irrigation demand rising
    -
      Region: Vietnam Central Highlands
      Risks: Water scarcity; robusta heat tolerance tested
  Map spec:
    Type: hexbin risk overlay

Slide 12: Implementation Roadmap + Stakeholder Callouts
Slide number: 12
Content:
  Phases:
    0–6 months:
      - Baseline: origin mapping, farm economics survey, emissions inventory (GHG+water).
      - Supplier code and NDPE/deforestation due diligence live.
      - Pilot: shade/soil program in 2 co-ops; roast energy audit.
    6–18 months:
      - Scale agroforestry + soil health to 30% volume; finance via pre-harvest loans.
      - Install wet mill water recirculation/biogas in 3 hubs.
      - Roaster upgrades (heat recovery) + renewable PPAs.
      - Packaging switch to lightweight recyclable across SKUs.
    18–36 months:
      - 90% traceable volumes; deforestation-free verified.
      - Ocean freight decarb commitments (slow steaming, carrier selection).
      - Supplier performance-linked premiums; public progress report.
  Workstreams:
    -
      Name: Farming & Resilience
      Owner: Sourcing + NGO partners
      Kpis:
        - Yield +10%
        - Shade coverage (ha)
    -
      Name: Traceability & Compliance
      Owner: Sustainability + Procurement
      Kpis:
        - % lots traceable
        - Deforestation risk cleared
    -
      Name: Roasting & Energy
      Owner: Ops/Engineering
      Kpis:
        - kWh/kg roasted
        - Scope 1–2 ↓
    -
      Name: Logistics & Packaging
      Owner: Supply Chain
      Kpis:
        - g CO2e/kg-km
        - Recycled content %
    -
      Name: Consumer & Brand
      Owner: Marketing
      Kpis:
        - Eco-label adoption
        - Education reach
  Stakeholder callouts:
    -
      Stakeholder: Farmers/Co-ops
      Ask: Adopt shade/soil/IPM; share plot data for MRV; receive premiums/inputs.
    -
      Stakeholder: Exporters/Importers
      Ask: Provide traceability docs; offer pre-finance; segregate lots.
    -
      Stakeholder: Roasters
      Ask: Invest in efficiency; shift to renewables; publish specs to suppliers.
    -
      Stakeholder: Retailers
      Ask: Commit to shelf standards; co-fund certifications; transparent pricing.
    -
      Stakeholder: Certifiers/NGOs
      Ask: Streamline audits; align metrics; support training.
    -
      Stakeholder: Financiers/DFIs
      Ask: Offer concessional credit and risk-sharing for smallholders.
    -
      Stakeholder: Governments
      Ask: Land-use enforcement; climate-smart extension; water stewardship.
  Timeline spec:
    Type: gantt-like
    Lanes:

      - Logistics/Packaging
      - Brand
